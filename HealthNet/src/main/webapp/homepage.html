What is Medicaid?

Medicaid is a state and federally funded program that provides health coverage for eligible low-income individuals and families. Covers essential medical services: doctor visits, hospital care, prescription medications, and preventive services.

Recent Changes

Missouri updated Medicaid eligibility requirements. New processes for verifying applicant information. Streamline access to healthcare and ensure accurate, timely enrollment

Employment Verification

Applicants may need to verify employment. Verification can be done by uploading pay stubs or allowing secure access to employer records. Ensures applicants meet updated eligibility requirements.
